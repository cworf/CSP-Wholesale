== Description ==

WooCommerce Customer Relationship Manager is an extension for WooCommerce that allows users to manage their business relationship with their customers. They can view the total spending and number of orders of each customers and the history of purchases. As well as view customers, they can email and bulk email customers.

== Usage ==

Simply upload the plugin and you're good to go. There are no options to configure - the extension just works.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire 'woocommerce-crm' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
